a note from me

i thought it was quite silly that people can use a reverse DNS domain like any other domain; the issue is that it's rather challenging to get control over such a zone. so I made this little site to give anyone who wants their own little ip6.arpa domain a chance to get one!
create an account, and you can immediately create a subdomain and add your own nameservers (that way you can add it to cloudflare or wherever!)

i maintain this service completely for free, no ads or anything, please consider supporting me through the donation options in the footer below c:
